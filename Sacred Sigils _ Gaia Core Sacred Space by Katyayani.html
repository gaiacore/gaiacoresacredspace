Understanding Sigils

What Is a Sigil?

A sigil is a sacred symbol created with a specific intention, purpose, and energetic frequency. Used across many spiritual traditions, sigils serve as powerful focal points for meditation, intention-setting, and energetic alignment. Your energy, your intention, your reality.

🧘
Meditate

Use your sigil as a daily meditation focal point. Gaze softly at the center, breathe deeply, and allow the energy of the symbol to work with your intention.

✨
Visualise

Place your sigil where you will see it daily — your altar, workspace, or screensaver. Each time you see it, your intention is reinforced and activated.

🌿
Align

Speak the affirmation aloud each morning. Allow the sigil, the words, and your breath to align your energy with the frequency you wish to embody.
