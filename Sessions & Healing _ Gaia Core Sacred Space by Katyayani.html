Your Experience

What to Expect

🌿
Before Your Session

Drink plenty of water, wear comfortable loose clothing, and avoid heavy meals directly beforehand. Come with an open mind and any questions or intentions you wish to set for your session.

✨
During Your Session

You are always in control. Sessions are conducted in a safe, nurturing environment. You may experience deep relaxation, emotional release, warmth, tingling sensations, or profound stillness — every experience is valid and unique.

🙏
After Your Session

Rest where possible, drink water, and be gentle with yourself. Integration can continue for several days after a session. You may receive follow-up guidance, meditation practices, or personalised recommendations.
